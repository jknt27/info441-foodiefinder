About Foodie Finder

"Foodie Finder" is an innovative application designed to connect food enthusiasts, providing a seamless solution for college students seeking quick, affordable meal options. Aimed initially at students at the University of Washington, especially incoming freshmen, "Foodie Finder" plans to expand to major U.S. cities. The application offers convenience and time-saving features for college students with hectic schedules, providing a single point of reference for all nearby restaurants. It's also a platform for fostering social connections among users with similar culinary interests.

We understand the unique challenges and opportunities of college life, not just in academics but also in personal growth and relationship building. Our commitment is to construct a sophisticated recommendation engine powered by the Yelp Fusion API, focusing on connecting food enthusiasts, fostering new friendships, and delivering easily accessible, affordable dining options. Our motivation stems from our own experiences as international and out-of-state students, aiming to create a memorable college journey for our peers.
